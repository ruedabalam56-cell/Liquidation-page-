How Crypto Liquidation Bot Works

Simple setup to automate your trading in minutes

1

Connect Your TradingView Alerts

Link your TradingView alerts via Telegram webhook or directly through our API. The bot will monitor and parse all incoming signals for the configured assets (BTC, SOL, SUI, ZEUS) on 30s charts.

2

Configure Your Exchange

Securely connect your exchange API keys (Binance, Bybit, OKX, etc.) with encrypted storage. Test the connection to ensure everything works properly.

3

Set Your Risk Parameters

Define your position sizing, stop-loss, and take-profit rules. Choose between percentage of balance or fixed amount risk per trade.

4

Activate the Bot

Start the bot and let it handle all trade execution automatically. Monitor performance through the dashboard and receive real-time notifications.
